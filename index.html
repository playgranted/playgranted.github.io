THE DJINN — Sacred Geometry Construct

Ethereal octahedron construct with energy tubes, holographic faces, orbital rings, and shader-driven core. Adapted from a sacred geometry energy matrix prototype.

THE GALAXY — Volumetric Spiral Background

Hubble-style spiral galaxy with FBM nebula clouds, rejection-sampled spiral arm particles, HII star-forming regions, dust lane silhouettes, oblate halo population, and billboarded galactic core.

Spiral arm math inspired by MRGRAVITY817/threejs-spiral-galaxy

Shader galaxy reference from AmitDigga/threejs-galaxy-shader

THE INTRO — Hyperspace Flythrough

Volumetric terrain raymarcher with FBM noise, cinematic tone mapping, and deceleration sequence. Full-screen shader quad rendered on a separate orthographic pass.

THE GAME

You make a wish. The Djinn grants it — exactly as worded. Then it exploits every gap, ambiguity, and missing qualifier to make the outcome devastating.

Your goal: craft a wish so airtight the Djinn can't find a loophole. If its twist is a stretch (8+/10), you win.

TIPS

Be specific. "I wish for money" leaves gaps — HOW you get it, legality, timing, consequences. The Djinn exploits what you forgot to say.

You have 200 characters. Every word matters.
